Get Involved

Your support puts the sun to work

Because cookers are priced below cost, every dollar reaches further. Here's how you can help Haitian families cook clean, safe, and affordably.

Donate

Fund clean cooking

Your gift funds demonstrations, training, and below-cost cookers for the households that need them most. PPAF is a UN Global Compact participant and a GlobalGiving vetted organization.

  • Sponsor a demonstration that introduces a whole community to solar cooking.
  • Help fund a cooker for a low-income family, school, or church.
  • Support local production that creates jobs in Haiti.
Donate securely → Ask about other ways to give

PPAF is a US 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Gifts are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.
